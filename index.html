About Us

WingCare Gujarat is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, treating, and protecting birds across Gujarat. We work to conserve bird habitats, spread awareness, and ensure every bird has a safe place to live

Our Services

Veterinary Treatment

Provide veterinary treatment and rehabilitation.

Release Birds

Release healthy birds back into their natural habitat.

Water Bowls

Install water bowls and bird feeders during summer.

Awareness

Conduct awareness programs in schools and communities.

Nest Boxes

Build and distribute nest boxes for birds.

Bird Rescue Helpline

Operate a bird rescue helpline for emergencies.

Report Injured Birds

Encourage people to report injured birds and support rescue efforts.

Mission

To protect birds, conserve their habitats, and inspire people to care for wildlife.
