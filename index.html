About Us

WingCare Gujarat is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, treating, and protecting birds across Gujarat. We work to conserve bird habitats, spread awareness, and ensure every bird has a safe place to live

Why Choose WingCare Gujarat?

Bird-First Care

Every rescued bird receives proper treatment and rehabilitation before returning to nature.

Compassionate Volunteers

Our volunteers are committed to protecting and caring for birds across Gujarat.

Nature Conservation

We encourage habitat protection through tree plantation, nest boxes, and clean surroundings.

Community Awareness

We inspire people to participate in bird conservation through awareness campaigns.

Our Services

Veterinary Treatment

Provide veterinary treatment and rehabilitation.

Release Birds

Release healthy birds back into their natural habitat.

Water Bowls

Install water bowls and bird feeders during summer.

Awareness

Conduct awareness programs in schools and communities.

Nest Boxes

Build and distribute nest boxes for birds.

Bird Rescue Helpline

Operate a bird rescue helpline for emergencies.

Report Injured Birds

Encourage people to report injured birds and support rescue efforts.

Our Mission

To protect birds, conserve their habitats, and inspire people to care for wildlife.

Our Vision

A future where every bird can live safely and fly freely.

How You Can Help Us?

Protect Bird Nests

Avoid disturbing bird nests and allow birds to raise their young safely.

Report Injured Birds

Report injured birds to rescue organizations so they can receive immediate care.

Keep Water Bowls

Place keep water bowls outside your home during summer to help thirsty birds.

Want to Become a Volunteer?

Join WingCare Gujarat and help protect birds across Gujarat. Whether it's rescuing injured birds, spreading awareness, or helping with conservation activities, every volunteer makes a difference.

Together, we can create a safer future for every bird.
